My favorite countries I have traveled to are Austria, Costa Rica, and Cape Verde

Jump to bottom

Austria

  1. The food was really cheap. I got a whole meal for $2.50
    • This included a sandwich, a bag of grapes, and a drink
  2. The architecture and history are AMAZING
    • The Schobrunn Palace Gardens were a favorite
    • The free walking tour made me appreciate the history of Austria
  3. It's not super touristy
  4. It's super clean
  5. Lots of pretty castles
    • I got a private tour of the Liechtenstein castle, which was built in 1130
    • Fortress Hohensalzburg has the best view of the city of Salzburg
  6. The hikes were absolutely stunning
    • Untersburg was the most difficult, yet most beautiful hike I have ever done

Costa Rica

  1. The surfing there was absolutely incredible
    • I was in my happy place
  2. Delicious Food
    • Exotic fruit, like Rambutans
    • Freshly caught fish
    • Sodas, their local restaurants, are very affordable
  3. The snorkeling is incredible
    • I got to hold an octopus and it inked in my hand
  4. PURA VIDAAAA
    • The culture is very relaxed and loving
  5. The weather is always warm
  6. Fall asleep to the rumbling of a thunderstorm every night

Cape Verde

  1. People call it the "Hawaii" of Africa
    • The water is warm and crystal blue
    • It's an island country
    • Lots of volcanic rock
  2. Rich in culture
    • When I was there, I went to a cultural parade and it was very unique
    • There was a fun rave on the beach
  3. It is pretty safe for an African country
    • I was in a group of about 15 and none of us got anything stolen over the course of 2 weeks in the country
  4. There are no mosquitoes so you do not run the risk of getting malaria or any other disease transferred through mosquitoes
  5. Lots of fun tours
    • Boat and fishing tour
    • Hiking
    • Unique shops
